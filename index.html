What We Offer

Calm, Responsible Pet Care

Four services tailored to your pet's personality and routine — always with patience, respect, and 20 years of experience.

🦮

Dog Walking

Walks in your neighbourhood tailored to your dog's energy level. Regular photo updates so you can follow along. Suitable for dogs up to 45 kg.

CHF 30 / hour
🏠

Boarding

Your dog stays at my home in a calm, non-smoking environment with cats present. Spayed/neutered dogs up to 18 kg. One family at a time — your dog gets full attention.

CHF 80 / night

House Sitting

I stay overnight in your home so your pets never leave their familiar surroundings. Feeding, routine, walks, and litter — everything covered while you're away.

CHF 80 / night

Drop-In Visits

Visits at your home for cats and dogs — feeding, litter, playtime, medication if needed, and a check-in with photos sent to you. No stress for your pet.

CHF 30 / visit
